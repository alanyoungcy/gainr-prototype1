# GAINR Protocol

**Gainr Protocol** is an AI-native, decentralized sports betting and prediction infrastructure built on **Solana**. It bridges the gap between professional trading syndicates (Web2) and decentralized finance (Web3) to solve the "Bettor's Dilemma"—the choice between unfair Web2 operators and illiquid Web3 protocols.

## Core Ecosystem (dApps)
Gainr powers three distinct native applications sharing the same liquidity and compliance stack:
- **Back.bet (The Consumer)**: A peer-to-pool sports betting platform. Features a 5-10% fee (vs industry 20%+) and eliminates "house risk".
- **Price.bet (The Trader)**: A prediction market for sports, politics, and fantasy trading, allowing analysts to monetize predictions.
- **Pick.bet (The Follower)**: A signal marketplace for copy-trading verified strategies, including the internal Gainr Analytics syndicate.

## Key Technical Pillars
- **Liquidity-as-a-Service (LaaS)**: Solves the "cold start" problem via a treasury-funded engine. A **Reserve Controller** automates liquidity injection to balance markets.
- **4-Layer AI Engine**: Developed with the University of Glasgow, featuring a "System 2" reasoning engine for deep EV analysis, not just a chatbot.
- **Compliance**: "Compliance-first" architecture using **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (zkMe)** for geo-fencing and age verification without compromising user privacy.

## Dual-Token Economy
- **$GBET (Internal Chip)**: Stablecoin-pegged (1:1 with USDC/EURC), used exclusively for wagering. Non-transferable Token-2022.
- **$GAINR (Utility Asset)**: Governance and utility token. 20% of protocol revenue buys back and burns $GAINR to drive deflationary value.

## Revenue & Roadmap
- **Revenue Model**: Platform rake (5-10%), AI SaaS subscriptions, and B2B APIs.
- **Roadmap**:
  - **Q4 2025 - Q1 2026**: Private Sale, MVP (Price.bet).
  - **Q2 2026**: Mainnet Launch (Back.bet), TGE, and UK/EU expansion.
  - **2027+**: Institutional adoption and Tier-1 CEX listings.

## Incubator
Incubated by **Gainr Analytics**, a professional sports trading firm, providing the protocol with proprietary data, real trading flow, and initial capital.
